Yamitsuki Cabbage Recipe

Ingredients:
200 g green cabbage, chopped into 1" pieces
½ tsp grated garlic
1 tsp Chinese-style chicken bouillon powder
½ Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
1 Tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Instructions:
Combine all ingredients and let it rest in fridge 15-60 minutes before serving. Best consumed same day. Store leftovers in fridge for up to 2 days.
